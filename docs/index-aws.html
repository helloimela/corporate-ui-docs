The font height of the bold font and the regular font are causing problems on the web, primarly when hovering or clicking links so that they change from Regular to Bold, or vice versa. The problem is that the Bold font is less tall than the Regular one. It gives a bad impression as the font are appearing to change. They do not look very similar.
